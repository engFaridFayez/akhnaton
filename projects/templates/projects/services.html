Our Services

Akhanton for Construction and Real Estate Development (ACRED) offers a full scope of building construction services, including Layouts, Architecture, Engineering Design and Project Management Services.

Description of services:

ACRED Engineers are highly experienced problem solvers with considerable in depth Knowledge of all construction materials and building structures. In- house computer systems assist our Structural Engineers in implementing your building design in concrete, structural steel or masonry.

ACRED multi – discipline capabilities provide an effective Project Management system. Our professionals manage all aspects of project coordination on Owner behalf, complement the client's staff.

ACRED objective, professional approach to design and construction control ensures that the client's completed building fulfills all expectations: financial, functional, and aesthetic.
